Mission

The study of shark behavior and movement patterns is essential for understanding their role in marine ecosystems and ensuring their conservation. Through this research, scientists can learn where, how, and why sharks move, which allows for the protection of both the species and the habitats they use.

About Us

We are a passionate, multidisciplinary student team dedicated to innovation and the creation of cutting-edge technological solutions for marine conservation. Our core mission is to design and develop advanced devices that significantly contribute to the research, protection, and preservation of shark communities in marine reserves worldwide.

Model

Our model collects information from datasets provided by NASA and oceanographic institutions. Using this data, we create correlations to predict shark movements and identify key migration patterns.
